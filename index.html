About

Sources is an abstract take on the concept of happy accidents. A world of chaos in which somehow the stars align. Training the viewer to have an exploratory vision of the world and how they see space.

Sources is inspired by the twentieth century art movements, Cubism and Abstract Art. Taking in various forms, and reimagining them through color, grid structure, angles, scale, and weight. Get lost as each piece straddles the line between perfection and chaos. The Sources collection is stored 100% on the Ethereum blockchain.

The Artist

Mason Dahl is a multidisciplinary designer and photographer based in Los Angeles. He has a bachelor’s degree in Graphic Design and is passionate about creating generative art, and the endless possibilities that we can achieve through technology.

Sources is Mason’s first NFT project and he feels grateful to be a new member of the community.
